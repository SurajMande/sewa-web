Our Mission

At SeWaa, our mission is to connect donors with NGOs and people in need. We make it easy for you to donate your unused items, such as clothes, shoes, and medicines, to those who require them the most. Together, we can make a significant impact and improve lives by repurposing items that would otherwise go unused.
