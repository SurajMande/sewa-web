Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate items?
You can donate items by navigating to our shop section, selecting a category like clothes, shoes, or medicines, and following the donation process. The NGOs will make requests based on the need, and you can fulfill their requests by donating accordingly.
What type of items can I donate?
You can donate extra clothes, shoes, medicines (within expiration limits), and other unused items that can help NGOs and child organizations. We ensure the items go to the right people in need.
How do I contact a specific NGO?
To contact a specific NGO, you can visit the NGO section on our website and choose the organization you're interested in.Their contact details and methods to connect with them will be displayed. You can reach out to them directly through the provided phone number or email address.
Can I donate internationally?
Yes, we accept international donations. You can contribute regardless of your location. We work with NGOs globally, so your donations will reach the right beneficiaries no matter where you are.
Are my donations tax-deductible?
Most donations are tax-deductible, depending on the laws in your country. After making a donation, you can request a receipt that may be used for tax purposes. Please consult a tax professional for specific details.
